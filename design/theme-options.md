# RIF Website Theme Options

Choose your preferred look and feel for the website.

## Option 1: Clean & Professional (Recommended)
**Style:** Modern, clean, professional, trustworthy

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** RIF Blue (#255eab)
- **Background:** White with subtle gray accents
- **Text:** Dark gray/black for readability
- **Accents:** Light blue highlights

### Characteristics
- Clean white backgrounds
- Generous white space
- Subtle shadows and borders
- Professional typography
- Blue accents for CTAs and links

**Best for:** Building trust, professional appearance, easy readability

## Design Elements to Choose

### Typography Style
- [ ] **Modern Sans-Serif** (Inter, system fonts) - Clean, professional
- [ ] **Classic Serif** (Georgia, serif) - Traditional, trustworthy
- [ ] **Bold Sans-Serif** (Montserrat, bold) - Strong, confident

### Button Style
- [ ] **Rounded** (rounded-lg) - Friendly, modern
- [ ] **Square** (rounded-none) - Professional, clean
- [ ] **Pill-shaped** (rounded-full) - Modern, playful

### Card Style
- [ ] **Flat** - Minimal shadows
- [ ] **Elevated** - Subtle shadows
- [ ] **Bordered** - Clear borders
- [ ] **Gradient** - Color gradients

### Spacing
- [ ] **Compact** - Tighter spacing
- [ ] **Comfortable** - Standard spacing
- [ ] **Generous** - Lots of white space

- Easy to read and navigate
- Works well for SEO content
