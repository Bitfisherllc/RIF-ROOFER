# RIF Color Palette

## Primary Colors (From Logo)

### RIF Blue (Primary Brand Color)
- **Hex:** `#255eab`
- **RGB:** `rgb(37, 94, 171)`
- **Usage:** Primary brand color, CTAs, links, highlights

### RIF Black
- **Hex:** `#231f20`
- **RGB:** `rgb(35, 31, 32)`
- **Usage:** Text, headers, dark accents

### White
- **Hex:** `#ffffff`
- **RGB:** `rgb(255, 255, 255)`
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Primary Actions
- Use RIF Blue (#255eab) for primary buttons and CTAs
- Use darker blue (#1e4a87) for hover states

### Text
- Primary text: RIF Black (#231f20) or Gray 900
- Secondary text: Gray 600 or Gray 700
- Text on dark: White (#ffffff)

### Backgrounds
- Primary background: White (#ffffff)
- Secondary background: Gray 50 or Gray 100
- Dark sections: RIF Black or Blue 900

### Borders
- Light borders: Gray 200
- Medium borders: Gray 300
- Dark borders: Gray 400
