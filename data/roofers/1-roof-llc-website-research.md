## About the Company

### Our Team
Jeremy Bleeker and his team have come from the post catastrophe restoration field and previously managed roofing and restoration companies throughout the united states, trained them how to work with insurance claims under extreme circumstances and to provide homeowners with timely quality repairs while meeting the highest standards. With over 75 years of combined experience, the team at 1 Roof are ready, willing and able to handle all your roofing needs.

**Key Team Member:**
- **Owner:** Jeremy Bleeker
- **Experience:** 75+ years combined experience
- **Background:** Post-catastrophe restoration field, managed roofing and restoration companies throughout the United States

### Our History
1 Roof stands for a singular vision bringing together our experience and expertise under 1 Roof to provide the best service possible for all of our customers. 

**Timeline:**
- **1998:** Jeremy started his first roofing company in Iowa
- **2012:** Jeremy and his family moved to Jacksonville (now called home)
- **October 7, 2016:** 1 Roof was formed after Hurricane Matthew devastated North Florida
- **Present:** Proudly serving the residents of NE Florida

**Notable Achievements:**
- Pioneered recycling of asphalt shingles
- Became a visionary for managed repair processes for the insurance restoration field
- Set out to make a positive impact on the roofing industry

### Our Specialties
Roofing and Restoration, Asphalt Shingle, Tile, Metal, Residential and Commercial are all systems that we offer installation, replacement or repair. We are Experts in the Insurance Claims Handling process, writing estimates using the same software insurance companies use. We offer Damage Recognition, Ladder Assistance and Emergency Tarping to adjusters and we welcome working together with insurance agents and adjusters to help expedite claims processing.
